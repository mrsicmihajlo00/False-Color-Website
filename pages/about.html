About Me

Mihajlo Mršić

Video Editor & Colorist

I am a video editor and colorist with a passion for visual storytelling. Based in Serbia, I work across commercial, documentary, film and music video formats — bringing a sharp eye for pacing, color, and emotion to every project.

My color work is grounded in DaVinci Resolve. Whether a project calls for a raw, naturalistic grade or a highly stylized cinematic palette, I adapt to the vision of each director and brand I collaborate with.

I am always open to new collaborations. If you have a project that needs a dedicated editor or colorist, let's talk.
